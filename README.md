# Comprehensive-Sales-Analysis-Report
A comprehensive, multi-page Power BI interactive dashboard designed to provide a 360-degree view of company performance. This project transforms raw data into actionable insights, covering financial health, product market trends, and human capital analytics from 2022 to 2024.

##  Questions (KPIs)

- What is the total revenue trend and sales growth between 2022 and 2024?

- Which geographic regions and months show the highest sales performance?

- What is the impact of the current discounting strategy on overall profit margins?

- Which product categories and specific items (like Cargo Bikes) drive the most revenue?

- How does sales volume flow across different channels (Retail, Wholesale) and customer segments?

## process 
- Data Connection: How was the raw data sourced and connected from multiple tables?

- Data Cleaning: How were null values, duplicates, and data types handled using Power Query?

- Data Modeling: How were the relationships (1:N) established between Sales, Products, and Staff tables

- DAX Calculations: What measures were created to calculate KPIs like Total Sales, Avg Discount, and YoY Growth?

- UI/UX Design: How was the multi-page navigation and color theme designed for a seamless user experience?

Interactivity: How were slicers (Year, Segment) and cross-filtering implemented for deep-dive analysis?
